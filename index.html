How it works

Each aerospace AI initiative is scored using six decision factors. The user controls the importance of each factor with sliders, and the final ranking updates immediately.

Final Score =
(Impact × Weight)
− (Risk × Weight)
− (Complexity × Weight)
+ (Feasibility × Weight)
− (Cost × Weight)
+ (Data Availability × Weight)
